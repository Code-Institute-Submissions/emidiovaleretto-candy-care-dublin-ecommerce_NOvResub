Candy Care provides a unique candy experience right to your door. We are experts in producing the best sweets, providing our friends and customers with an incredible experience. Based in Dublin, Ireland, we are dedicated to providing you with the best candy in the world.

Brigadeiro

This famous sweet is a type of Brazilian Chocolate Confectionery. It's a scrumptious bite-size (or two) chocolate sweet, very popular in Brazil. It is not a bonbon, it is not a truffle, fudge, caramel, brownie, or a cupcake... It's Brigadeiro!
