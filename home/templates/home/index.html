Brigadeiro

This famous sweet is a type of Brazilian Chocolate Confectionery. It's a scrumptious bite-size (or two) chocolate sweet, very popular in Brazil. It is not a bonbon, it is not a truffle, fudge, caramel, brownie, or a cupcake... It's Brigadeiro!
